> 英语语法

## Time Clause

### 一般现在时 +  一般将来时 

- 句式：`时间介词`+`一般现在时`+`,`+`一般将来时`
- 含义：表示某事一定会发生
- 时间介词：
    - **When**
        - When I get home, I will wash my hands.
    - **As soon as**
        - As soon as the lesson ends, I will go home.
    - **Before**
        - Before it gets dark, I will walk the dog
    - **After**
        - After this programme ends, I'm going to bed
    - **Until**
